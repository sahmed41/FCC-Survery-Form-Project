A.J.P. Abdul Kalam

Aboud Him

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was the 11th president of India who is also an aerospace scientist. He was also a science administrator at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). He took part in India's civilian space programme and military missile development effort. He was known as the Missile Man of India for working on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He also played several roles in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.

Highlights of His Life

  • 1931 - Bon on 15th of October in Rameswara
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from Saint Joseph's College,Tiruchirappalli.
  • 1955 - Moved to Madras to study aerospace engineering in Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1960 - Graduated from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1963 to 1964 - Visited NASA's Langley Research Center inHampton, Virginia; Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland; and Wallops Flight Facility.
  • 1965 - Started work on an expandable rocket project independently at DRDO.
  • 1969 - Transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  • 1970s - Directed two projects called Project Devil and Project Valiant, which sought to develop ballistic missiles from the technology of the successful SLV programme.
  • 1970s and 1990s - Made an effort to develop the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) and SLV-III projects.
  • 1992 to 1999 - Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and Secretary of the Defence Research and Development Organisation.
  • 1998 - Developed a low cost coronary stent, named the "Kalam-Raju Stent" along with cardiologist Soma Raju.
  • 2002 - Won the PResidential election.
  • 2002 to 2007 - Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2012 - Designed a rugged tablet computer for health care in rural areas along with cardiologist Soma Raju, which was named the "Kalam-Raju Tablet".
  • 2012 - Launched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement, with a central theme of defeating corruption.
  • 2015 - He passed away.
